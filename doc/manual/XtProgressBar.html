__Direction - direction the progress is going, which can be:

PB_LEFT_TO_RIGHT left to the right

PB_RIGHT_TO_LEFT right to the left

PB_BOTTOM_TO_TOP bottom to the top

PB_TOP_TO_BOTTOM top to the bottom
